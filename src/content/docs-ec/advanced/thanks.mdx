## 开源参考项目

本主题使用/参考/借鉴了以下项目的内容：

框架：

- [astro](https://github.com/withastro/astro)
- [unocss](https://github.com/unocss/unocss)

内容渲染：

- [mdx](https://github.com/mdx-js/mdx/)
- [@unocss/preset-typography](https://github.com/unocss/unocss/tree/main/packages-presets/preset-typography)
- [@unifiedjs/rehype](https://github.com/rehypejs/rehype)
- [@unifiedjs/remark](https://github.com/remarkjs/remark)

集成工具：

- 代码块：[Shiki](https://github.com/shikijs/shiki)
- 图片灯箱：[Medium Zoom](https://github.com/francoischalifour/medium-zoom)
- 评论：[Waline](https://github.com/walinejs/waline)
- 搜索：[Pagefind](https://github.com/cloudcannon/pagefind)

其他：

- 压缩工具：[playform/compress](https://github.com/PlayForm/Compress)
- 图片压缩：[sharp](https://github.com/lovell/sharp)
